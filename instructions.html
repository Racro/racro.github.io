Email Guidelines

Before sending an email, please note:

  • Please include a clear subject line indicating your purpose
  • Introduce yourself briefly
  • Be specific about what you're looking for
  • If you're an undergraduate student interested in research, please mention:
    • Your current year and major
    • Relevant coursework or projects
    • Specific areas of interest
    • Your availability and timeline

I aim to respond to all emails within 48 hours. Thank you for your understanding!
